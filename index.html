Our Philosophy

Where fire
meets finesse

At Ember & Oak, we believe great food begins with honest ingredients and ancient techniques. Our open hearth burns slow — oak, cherry, hickory — and every dish that passes through its heat carries that story.

Chef Arjun Mehta draws from years cooking across Lyon, Osaka, and Mumbai — translating those memories into a menu that is both rooted and restless.
